Sensing capabilities

Motion

  • 3-axis gyroscope with ranges of ±250 dps, ±500 dps, ±1000 dps, and ±2000 dps
  • 3-axis accelerometer with ranges of ±2g, ±4g, ±8g, and ±16g
  • 3-axis compass with a wide range to ±4900 µT

AFE

  • Standalone ECG up to 4 kHz
  • Synchronized Photoplethysmogram (PPG) and ECG up to 1 kHz

Environment

  • Temperature with range of –10°C to 100°C, accurate to ±1°C
  • Piezoresistive pressure sensor (altimeter) with range of 260 to 1260 hPa

Bioimpedance

  • Voltage, current, and impedance
